Workshops & Trainings

Are you looking to move your organization to the next level of D&I? Our menu of workshops and trainings aim to increase your level of cultural awareness, confidence, competence, skills, and organizational effectiveness around issues of identity, belonging, and inclusion.

Uncomfortable Conversations: How to Talk About Race in Your Space

How can you facilitate conversations with employees/students/colleagues about one of the most uncomfortable topics in US society—race? This session will provide you with tools and real life examples to engage with race and racism in your workplace or classroom.

Creating Your Brand

We all know that creating your professional brand is important, but how do you create yours? In this session, you will create professional core values and mission statements, learn strategies to develop professional relationships, and how to stand out at work for all the right reasons.

Cultural Authenticity at Work

Code-switching. Conservative hair and dress. Anglo accents. Name changes. These are just a few tactics that women, people of color, LGBT people, and others deploy to fit into workplace culture that penalizes our identities. The offhand comments, private conversations, and the promotion of our assimilated colleagues--all in the midst in increasing conversations about the authenticity in the workplace-- make us question who can truly be themselves at work. In this workshop, you will learn strategies to be more culturally authentic at work.

Employee Resource Groups

Employee resource groups (also called “affinity groups”) are a vital part of an organization’s commitment to an inclusive culture. But how do you create and sustain the necessary ERG’s for your organization to thrive? In this workshop, you will learn how to assess your organization’s ERG needs as well as strategies to sustain them for underrepresented groups and allies.

Organizational Change

While personal awareness is a necessary starting point for diversity and inclusion, the ultimate goal is diversity and inclusion recruitment, retention, and advancement efforts that are sustainable and embedded into an organization. This workshop covers the various stages of diversity and inclusion maturity and provides time to brainstorm strategic 3-5 year plans for your organization.
