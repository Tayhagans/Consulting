ABOUT

Most people want to promote diversity and inclusion, but have no idea how to do it. Moreover, many organizations are culturally diverse, but fail at inclusion because their policies and culture do not promote the retention and advancement of underrepresented groups.

In 2018, Zahida Sherman founded Crown Consulting & Co. to help organizations and individuals promote diversity and inclusion. Crown Consulting & Co. leverages Zahida’s experience as a diversity and inclusion professional with expertise in higher education and the legal field to promote equity, access, recruitment, retention, and advancement across industries. She has a B.A. from Ithaca College in anthropology and Africana Studies and her M.A. in African history with a minor in African American Studies from Northwestern University. Zahida’s writing focuses on race, gender, adulthood, and popular culture. Her work has been published in Bustle, xoJane, Healthline, Blavity, ForHarriet, Revelist and How to Help, among others.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do all meetings have to take place in person?
No! To accommodate your schedule, we offer meetings online via Zoom.
How much do services cost?
Pricing depends on the type service you are requesting, the size of the group, materials required, as well as travel and accommodation required. Typically, individual consulting starts at $125/hr and a 2-3 hr departmental training or workshop starts at $750.
Do you offer custom workshops?
Yes! Contact me with an idea of what topics you would like covered, and we’ll work together to develop a workshop specific to your role and field.
Will you speak at my conference/write for my publication?
I am happy to learn more about the project that you have in mind! Please contact me with the details.
Can I use your writing on my site?
Not without my permission. To syndicate my writing, please get in touch with what you are interested in using, where, and the audience. If I do not respond, please assume the article is not available for you.
