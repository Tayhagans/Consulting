One - on - One Coaching

Attending a diversity and inclusion training is easy. Implementing the tools and lessons learned from the training is the hard part. If this sounds like you, work one on one with a coach to dive into lingering questions, challenges, concerns, or material to better implement your diversity and inclusion needs.

Leading and Managing

These sessions will teach you how to lead and manage your organization with confidence. We will focus on boosting your cultural competency, tackling your D&I “blind spots,” creating a strategic plan, and how to discuss D&I authentically to stakeholders.

Creating Buy-in

Stakeholder engagement with diversity and inclusion can be difficult, especially with multiple opinions and competing levels of cultural competence in an organization. These sessions will cover strategies to use with stakeholders and ways to frame diversity and inclusion as win-win.

Nurturing Allies, Creating Champions

One of the most difficult tasks for an organization is to train those at the top—who are often from the most privileged groups—to advocate for underrepresented groups in their organization. This session will give you the tools and confidence to be a voice at the decision-making table for underrepresented colleagues, employees, and students in your organization.

Overcoming Roadblocks

Diversity and inclusion work is difficult and it can take many years to see the fruits of your commitment. So how do you maintain your commitment and desire to move forward? Work with a coach to put strategic goals in perspective, learn how to celebrate small victories, and rework plans, where necessary.
